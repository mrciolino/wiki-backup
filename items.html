Shard of Divinity

A fragment of divine power, said to grant godlike abilities.

Mechanical Mirror Shield

A shield that can reflect any ray attack back at the attacker. Can be found at Mechanical Mirror Shield on DnD Beyond or its Wiki Page.

Godsbane

A sword capable of slaying deities and other divine beings. Godsbane was destroyed when Bazzdos casted “Mordenkainen's Disjunction” on the blade in an attempt to destroy Ceric. 

Arc of Death

A tome containing spells that bring instant death to foes.

Rocket Gauntlets

Gauntlets that provide enhanced strength and the ability attack with ranged or melee attacks. Can be found at Rocket Gauntlets on DnD Beyond or on its Wiki Page.
