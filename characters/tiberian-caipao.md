#### **[Tiberian Caipao](/characters/tiberian-caipao)**  
- **Race:** Dragonborn  
- **Class:** Artificer  
- **Background:** Noble Weaponsmith  
- **Key Traits:** Ambitious, resourceful, haunted by his past, gambling addiction.  
- **Backstory:** Tiberian hails from the [Rascal Clan](/organizations/rascal-clan), a prominent family in the arms trade of [Vrintia](/locations/vrintia). His parents were murdered by the rival Clan Norexias, forcing him to flee and fabricate a criminal past to escape their grasp. He now seeks to reclaim his family’s honor and legacy while navigating the dangers of the [Royal Vrintia Academy of Swords, Song, and Sorcery (RVASSS)](/organizations/rvasss).  
- **Relationships:**  
  - **Party:** Tiberian has formed a strong bond with the party, particularly after revealing his vendetta against Clan Norexias. His relationship with [Thomas Stonebrook](/characters/thomas-stonebrook) was initially strained due to accusations of murder, but they have since reconciled.  
  - **NPCs:** Tiberian has a complicated relationship with [Professor Ozdemir](/npcs/professor-osdemir), who serves as a mentor, and a deep-seated hatred for Clan Norexias. He also has a former bodyguard, Kreiterax, who remains a loyal ally.  
  - **Organizations:** Tiberian’s ties to the [Rascal Clan](/organizations/rascal-clan) and his enrollment at [RVASSS](/organizations/rvasss) place him at the center of political and economic intrigue in [Vrintia](/locations/vrintia).  
- **Notable Actions/Dialogue:**  
  - **[Session 1](/session/session-1):** Tiberian is falsely accused of murder via a forged wanted poster, but the party later discovers it was an illusion. He uses his breath weapon and strength to fend off cultists, earning the party’s trust.  
  - **[Session 5](/session/session-5):** Tiberian reveals his vendetta against Clan Norexias and attempts to warn the professors about a suspicious Brass Dragonborn infiltrator.  
- **Emotional Arcs:**  
  - **From Suspect to Ally:** Tiberian shifts from being a suspected criminal to a trusted ally after the party learns the truth about his past.  
  - **Reclaiming Honor:** Tiberian is determined to reclaim his family’s legacy and seeks revenge against Clan Norexias, which drives much of his emotional growth.  
- **Progression:**  
  - **[Session 0](/session/session-0):** Tiberian’s backstory as a noble weaponsmith is fleshed out, giving him ties to the arms trade in [Vrintia](/locations/vrintia).  
  - **[Session 3](/session/session-3):** Tiberian explores the [Wizard Tower](/locations/wizard-tower), uncovering Gilbert’s writings linked to demon summoning, and gains favor with [Master Tanu](/npcs/master-tanu).  
  - **[Session 5](/session/session-5):** Tiberian is sorted into the School of Sorcery, Sign of the Dragon, marking a new chapter in his journey.
