Gilembror

The protector of the Forge Dimension inside of the Realm of Fire. Gilembror is a stone statue who keeps a watchful eye over the forge.

Origin

Was first met by Bazzdos when he enterted the Forge Dimension for the first time. 

Gilembror's Appeasement

For Bazzdos to use the forge, he first needed to prove he was a capable smith. Gilembror's Appeasement are cloth gloves which proved Bazzdos' capability and gained him the ability to use the Forge Dimension.
