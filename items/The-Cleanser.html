A Greatsword used by Spoops during his time as an Oath of the Watchers Paladin among the High Elves of Ereriad. 

Origin

First given to Spoops when he was an Oath of the Watchers Paladin among the High Elves of Ereriad. Spoops gave his “life” and used the blade to seal the serpent Jormungandr within the elven city in Ereriad. Unfortunately the city sank beneath the waves. 

During the campaign, the party found the blade, breaking the seal and releasing jormungandr. The serpent was quickly re-sealed within the Jormungandr Diamond.
