A fragment of Spoops' Divine power. This blade is a part of Spoops very being. The blade itself or a copy can be granted to another by Spoops' will.

Origin

The Shard of Divinity was forged over the course of 15,000 years in various time-dilated chambers, by reforging multiple powerful blades gathered and forged by Spoops and Bazzdos into a single ultimate weapon while fusing this new blade with Spoops himself.
