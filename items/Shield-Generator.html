Part of a set of magical items, the other item being the Shield Ring. Allows the generation and manipulation of an impenetrable shield.

Origin

Part of a set of two magical items found in the tomb of Tiamani the Awesome. The ring was worn by Spoops, and the Shield Generator was used by Os.

This item was surgically and magically implanted on Os Vrntica’s heart during the battle of Fywick. As the rest of the party held off the assault, the lich Ralindu, Freedom, and the Oracle of Fywick performed the procedure to implant the device in Os. Once the device was implanted, Os began his most brilliant performance yet, manifesting the Shield around the city, keeping the waves of monsters at bay while allowing the party and Fywick’s forces to clean up anything inside the barrier. Once Darmuth’s Creature reached the shield, Os managed to keep it at bay long enough for Spoops wearing the Shield Ring to leap spinning into the mouth of the monster, tearing it up from the inside, finishing the fight.
