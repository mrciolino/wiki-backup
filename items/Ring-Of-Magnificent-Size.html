A ring studded with 10 Diamonds, each containing a charge of Enlarge-Reduce. The ring has the ability to stack castings of Enlarge-Reduce on whatever the spell is applied to. The castings of the spell do not require concentration. 

Origin

The ring was crafted by Bazzdows and Spoops during their second “Time Dilated“ Forging Experience. They crafted a magical platinum ring capable of containing 10 charges of Enlarge-Reduce. Each charge is contained within one of ten diamonds which stud the band. Spoops instructed Bazzdows on how to inscribe the magical runes that define the Enlarge-Reduce spell into the diamonds.
