Gilembror's Appeasement

A cloth glove forged with the help of Gilembror inside of Bazzdos' Forge Dimension in the Realm of Fire above a volcano. Worn by Bazzdos.

Origin

When Bazzdos first received the ring that allowed for travel via door portal to the realm of fire, the stone statue of Gilembror was the first person he met inside. To unlock the power of the forge, Bazzdos had to prove himself a capable worksmith to get the support of Gilembror. The creation of this item was that trail.
