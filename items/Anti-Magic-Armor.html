Anti Magic Armor which provides immunity to Magic when activated.

Origin

The armor was first obtained by Spoops when he used a wish he gained from the Deck of Many Things to wish for “Each member of the party to gain a Legendary item most suitable for them”. This granted Spoops the armor.

After gaining am epic boon, Spoops fused with the armor. The armor became part of Spoops, making his bones appear platinum. Spoops can manifest the armor at will, and he passively benefits from its effects even when he doesn’t appear to be wearing it. 

After ascending to godhood, Spoops gained the ability to lend his Soulbound items to others. He is able to lend a copy of this armor to someone wherever they may be .
