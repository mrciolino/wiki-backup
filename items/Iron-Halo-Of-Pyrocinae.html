A holy relic, the Iron Halo was once blessed by the Goddess Of fire Pyrocinae, granting it the power to emit her holy fire, allowing the wearer to deal fire damage to any who dare approach.

Origin

The halo was obtained by Spoops when he stole it from its display in the Library of the magical College of Highhold. Later he asked the Goddess Pyrocinae to allow him the usage of her blessing.
