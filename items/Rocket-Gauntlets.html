Rocket Gauntlets

The signature weapon of Bazzdos. Has 3 use cases: ranged weapon attack, melee weapon attack, and as a utility tool.

Origins

Upon finding Bazzdos in a cave under ENTER CITY HERE, Bazzdos wielded the Unimproved Rocket Gauntlets before they were upgraded during the 2nd Time Chamber Forge session with Spoops.
