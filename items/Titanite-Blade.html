The Titanite Blade was an incredibly powerful sword wielded by Spoops after obtaining it from the titan Iaeptus.

Origin

The blade was given to Spoops by Iaeptus after Spoops accidentally helped Bane free Iaeptus from his prison within the planet Dolarion. The Iaeptus took a massive chunk of titanite which had once made up part of his cage, and molded it into the blade barehanded.
