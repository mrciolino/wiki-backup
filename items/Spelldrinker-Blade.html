Up to six spells can be stored in the blade. When you successfully hit a creature with this sword, you may release the stored spells, causing the hit creature to automatically be effected by all spells without chance to save.

Origin

The blade was originally obtained by Adalynn.

later is was used by Spoops during the final war against the Ithillids. Spoops imburd The blade with the 12th level spell Divine Command which he attempted to use to kill the Corrupted Ceremorph Zeus. However their immunity to being targeted by attacks prevented this. He then used the blade on the Emperor, successfully subjugating them and allowing Breezy-e to deal the death blow.
