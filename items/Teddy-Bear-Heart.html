The Teddy Bear Heart of Spoops, looking into the eyes of the  teddy bear has a calming effect. It has the power to charm those who look at it. 

Origin

While investigating Demsford, Spoops found a Teddy bear that seemed to have a calming effect when looking into its eyes. He found out that it this magical effect was due to it containing the soul of a child named Teddy.
